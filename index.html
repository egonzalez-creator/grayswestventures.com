Founder & Managing Principal

Edmundo Gonzalez is a company founder, executive, and investor with more than two decades of experience building technology-enabled businesses in healthcare and logistics. He began his career at PricewaterhouseCoopers as a management consultant before moving into operating roles as COO at TestU and VP of Sales & Marketing at STI (later Chalkable).

From 2007 to 2014, Edmundo was a Partner at Selway Capital, managing a portfolio of venture-backed technology companies — co-leading exits including DVTel (sold to FLIR Systems for $100M), Magnolia Broadband (sold to Google for $30M), and Chalkable (sold to PowerSchool for $27M). He subsequently served as Operating Partner at CNTP, a $300M VC fund, co-leading a $103M Israeli sub-fund.

In 2019, Edmundo co-founded Marpai, an AI-driven administrator of self-insured healthcare plans. As CEO, he raised private capital, executed a NASDAQ IPO in 2021 raising $28.75M, and grew net revenues to over $37M by 2023. Through Grays West, he also led the sale of Nuvem to Parthenon Capital — delivering a return of over 25× for shareholders.
